What I do?

Here are some of my expertise

Project Initiation

  • Scope of work is discussed; client's preliminary needs are assessed
  • Required documents Submission
  • Detailed meeting with assigned Architect and Engineer
  • Site is visited, surveyed & inspected by the project manager, engineering team and architects

Planning and design

  • Client requirements are finalized and project viability is assessed
  • First draft submission, mood board and plan confirmation
  • Furniture layouts, 3D views and walkthroughs
  • Design presentation on-site with VR experience

Detail Design

  • Sub-structure and super structure drawings.
  • Structural design, MEP and other alliance consultations
  • Material specifications and selections
  • Interior and landscape detail drawings

Project Monitoring

  • Monitor project progress against the drawings
  • Review and record revisions from initial plan with the client in meetings
  • Conduct periodic quality checks and ensure the project is aligned with the proposed design
