Welcome

Who we are

Whitebrick is a dynamic Kerala-based firm offering a spectrum of services, including architectural design, interior, and landscape design, as well as supervision. Specialising in Commercial, Residential, Hospitality, and Retail projects, we are dedicated to collaboration, blending economy, function, and aesthetics in our designs. Our diverse talent pool and external partnerships enable us to tackle projects of any scale, focusing primarily on tropical modern style while exploring various design aesthetics

Design Philosophy

At Whitebrick, our philosophy centres on transcending the ordinary by prioritising collaboration to tailor designs to meet unique client needs. Rooted in the tropical modern style, our versatile approach seamlessly integrates different styles into projects, ensuring a dynamic and personalised outcome.
